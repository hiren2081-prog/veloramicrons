Who We Are

Built on Purity.
Driven by Precision.

Velora Microns LLP is a modern calcium carbonate processing plant based in Morbi, Gujarat — the industrial heartland of India. We manufacture Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC) and Precipitated Calcium Carbonate (PCC) with consistent particle size, superior brightness, and reliable supply.

Our plant is equipped with advanced micronizing and surface-coating technology, ensuring every batch meets the strict demands of industrial buyers. From valve-bag packaging to bulk tanker supply, we serve B2B clients with professionalism and speed.
