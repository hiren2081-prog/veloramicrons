Normal Ground Calcium Carbonate (GCC)

Velora Microns' Ground GCC is produced by dry grinding high-purity limestone to precise micron sizes. Available in a wide mesh range from 100 to 2500, our GCC offers high brightness and consistent quality for paints, paper, adhesives and construction.

Our advanced micronizing technology ensures tight particle size distribution, uniform whiteness and low grit content in every batch.

Applications

🎨

Paints & Coatings

GCC acts as an extender pigment in paints, improving opacity, consistency and reducing cost. Fine grades (VG-500 to VG-1250) provide smooth finish in emulsion and enamel paints.

📄

Paper & Board

Used as coating pigment and filler in paper, GCC improves brightness, opacity and printability. Fine GCC grades replace expensive TiO₂ in paper coating.

🏠

Construction Materials

Coarse GCC grades are widely used in putty, tile adhesives, cement boards and waterproofing compounds to improve workability and reduce shrinkage.

🧴

Adhesives & Sealants

GCC improves rheology, thixotropy and reduces cost in adhesives and sealants while maintaining bond strength. Grades VG-325 to VG-800 are most suitable.

🏺

Ceramics & Tiles

Used as flux material in ceramic tile bodies and glazes. GCC improves dimensional stability and reduces firing temperature, saving energy costs.

🌾

Agriculture

Coarse GCC grades are used for soil pH correction and as calcium supplement in fertilizers. Improves crop yield and soil structure.

Product 03 — Flagship

VELORA COAT® — Coated Ground GCC

VELORA COAT® is our premium surface-treated calcium carbonate, coated with stearic acid for superior dispersibility and hydrophobicity. Designed for polymer, PVC and masterbatch applications where performance matters most.

The stearic acid coating creates a hydrophobic surface that improves compatibility with polymer matrices, reduces moisture absorption and allows higher filler loading without compromising mechanical properties.

Applications

🧴

Plastics & Polymers

VELORA COAT® allows polymers to heat and cool faster, improving productivity. Higher filler loading reduces raw material cost while maintaining mechanical strength. Grades: VC-20T to VC-50T.

🏠

PVC Pipes & Fittings

CaCO₃ improves stiffness and impact resistance of PVC pipes. Smaller particle sizes work best for larger diameter pipes with 2–3 micron formulations. Grades: VC-30T, VC-40T, VC-50T.

🔌

PVC Cables & Wires

Stearic acid coated GCC increases insulating effect in PVC cable compounds. Improves processability and reduces dielectric constant. Grades: VC-50T, VC-60T.

🎨

Masterbatch

Most polyolefin fabricators use CaCO₃ via masterbatches at 70–80% loading. VELORA COAT® provides excellent dispersion in polyolefin carrier resin. Grades: VC-20T, VC-30T.

🧵

Raffia & Nonwoven

Higher thermal conductivity and lower specific heat of CaCO₃ are key advantages in raffia tape and nonwoven fabric applications, reducing costs significantly. Grades: VC-50T, VC-60T.

🚗

Rubber Applications

Ground CaCO₃ acts as extender and detacktifying agent in rubber. Adds stiffness and provides abrasion resistance in automotive and industrial rubber products. Grades: VC-60T, VC-70T.
